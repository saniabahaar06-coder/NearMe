Queens Land in Chennai is a sprawling 70-acre amusement and water park on the Chennai-Bangalore highway, offering a mix of thrilling dry rides (like roller coasters, Free Fall), water attractions (wave pool, water slides), a kids' play area, and green spaces, serving as a popular family entertainment spot with numerous rides (adult/kids) included in the entry fee, though food quality and queue times can vary, as per Tripadvisor reviews, says one user, notes another reviewer.
